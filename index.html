六大时期Six Periods

音乐时代概览Musical Eras at a Glance

每个时代的音乐都折射出当时的社会思潮、宗教信仰与人文精神。The music of each era reflects the social currents, religious beliefs, and humanistic spirit of its time.

Renaissance

文艺复兴时期The Renaissance

约 1400 — 1600 年c. 1400 — 1600

以人文主义精神为核心，音乐从宗教礼仪中缓缓走向世俗。复调音乐（Polyphony）达到巅峰，多声部和声织体精妙绝伦，弥撒曲与经文歌构成了这一时代最壮阔的声音图景。Guided by humanist ideals, music gradually moved from sacred liturgy toward the secular. Polyphony reached its zenith — exquisitely woven multi-voice textures in masses and motets formed the grandest soundscape of the age.

若斯坎·德普雷Josquin des Prez 帕莱斯特里纳Palestrina 奥兰多·迪·拉索Orlando di Lasso 威廉·伯德William Byrd
♬
Baroque

巴洛克时期The Baroque

约 1600 — 1750 年c. 1600 — 1750

装饰繁复、情感强烈、对比鲜明。数字低音（Basso Continuo）奠定和声基础，协奏曲、奏鸣曲与歌剧相继诞生。巴赫的对位、亨德尔的宏大、维瓦尔第的活泼，共同塑造了这个辉煌时代。Ornate, emotionally intense, and rich in contrast. The basso continuo laid harmonic foundations while the concerto, sonata, and opera emerged. Bach's counterpoint, Handel's grandeur, and Vivaldi's vivacity together forged this magnificent era.

约翰·塞巴斯蒂安·巴赫J. S. Bach 亨德尔Handel 维瓦尔第Vivaldi 蒙特威尔第Monteverdi 帕赫贝尔Pachelbel
𝄢
Classical

古典主义时期The Classical Period

约 1750 — 1820 年c. 1750 — 1820

追求均衡、清晰与理性的美学秩序，奏鸣曲式（Sonata Form）成为最重要的结构原则。维也纳古典乐派三杰——海顿、莫扎特与贝多芬——将交响曲、弦乐四重奏与钢琴奏鸣曲推向了艺术的高峰。Pursuing balance, clarity, and rational aesthetic order, sonata form became the paramount structural principle. The three Viennese masters — Haydn, Mozart, and Beethoven — elevated the symphony, string quartet, and piano sonata to their artistic zenith.

莫扎特Mozart 海顿Haydn 贝多芬Beethoven 克莱门蒂Clementi
♩
Romantic

浪漫主义时期The Romantic Era

约 1820 — 1900 年c. 1820 — 1900

个人情感、民族精神与文学幻想统摄一切。管弦乐规模空前扩大，标题音乐与交响诗蓬勃兴起。钢琴成为浪漫主义的灵魂乐器，肖邦与李斯特为其写下了最深情的诗篇。Personal emotion, national spirit, and literary fantasy reigned supreme. Orchestras expanded to unprecedented scale, and programme music and the symphonic poem flourished. The piano became Romanticism's soul instrument, with Chopin and Liszt composing its most heartfelt poetry.

肖邦Chopin 舒曼Schumann 勃拉姆斯Brahms 李斯特Liszt 柴可夫斯基Tchaikovsky 瓦格纳Wagner
♫
Impressionism

印象主义时期Impressionism

约 1890 — 1920 年c. 1890 — 1920

受莫奈、马拉美等视觉艺术家启发，音乐开始描绘光影与色彩的瞬息变化。德彪西解放了和声，打破传统调性束缚，用音色与音响本身作为表达媒介，创造出朦胧而精致的声音世界。Inspired by Monet, Mallarmé, and other visual artists, music began to depict the fleeting play of light and color. Debussy liberated harmony, broke free of traditional tonality, and used timbre itself as a medium of expression, creating a hazy yet exquisite sonic world.

德彪西Debussy 拉威尔Ravel 福雷Fauré 萨蒂Satie
≈
Modern & Beyond

现代主义时期The Modern Era

1910 年至今1910 — Present

调性体系被彻底颠覆。十二音技法、无调性音乐、序列主义相继登场。斯特拉文斯基以原始节奏震惊世界，勋伯格重写了音乐的语法，而简约主义则在喧嚣中寻回一份禅意的静谧。The tonal system was utterly overturned. Twelve-tone technique, atonality, and serialism took the stage in turn. Stravinsky shocked the world with primal rhythms, Schoenberg rewrote the grammar of music, and Minimalism found Zen-like stillness amid the noise.

斯特拉文斯基Stravinsky 勋伯格Schoenberg 肖斯塔科维奇Shostakovich 巴托克Bartók 菲利普·格拉斯Philip Glass
